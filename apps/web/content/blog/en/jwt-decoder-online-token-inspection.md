# JWT Decoder Online for Fast Token Inspection

A **jwt decoder online** lets you inspect payload claims during debugging without building a custom script.

## What you can check

- `exp` expiration timestamps
- `iss` issuer values
- `sub` subject identifiers
- Custom authorization claims

## Safe usage tips

Use a decoder for reading only. Signature verification still belongs in your authentication stack.

## Common workflows

Developers use a JWT decoder online to troubleshoot callback flows, verify staging tokens, and explain auth payloads to teammates.
