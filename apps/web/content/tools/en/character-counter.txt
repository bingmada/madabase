[FAQ]
What does character counter do?
A character counter counts the number of characters in your text, including or excluding spaces depending on your needs.

Why are character limits important?
Social media platforms limit posts (Twitter/X: 280 chars), search engines truncate meta descriptions, and database fields have storage limits.

What is the difference between character count with and without spaces?
Some limits count all characters including spaces; others count only visible characters. Knowing both helps you meet different requirements.

How many characters for SEO meta descriptions?
Search engines typically display 150-160 characters of meta descriptions. Keeping descriptions under 160 characters ensures they display fully.

What is Twitter character limit?
Twitter (now X) allows 280 characters per tweet, but this includes links which are automatically shortened.

Can I count characters for Unicode text?
Yes. The counter handles Unicode characters including emoji, CJK characters, and special symbols correctly.

Is my text uploaded?
No. All counting happens locally in your browser.
