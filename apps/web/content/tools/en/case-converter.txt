[FAQ]
What case formats are supported?
Common formats include: lowercase, UPPERCASE, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, and kebab-case.

What is camelCase?
camelCase combines words with the first word lowercase and subsequent words capitalized. Example: helloWorldExample. Common in JavaScript variable naming.

What is snake_case?
snake_case uses lowercase letters with underscores separating words. Example: hello_world_example. Common in Python and SQL.

What is kebab-case?
kebab-case uses lowercase letters with hyphens separating words. Example: hello-world-example. Common in URLs and CSS.

What is Title Case?
Title Case capitalizes the first letter of each major word. Example: Hello World Example. Common in titles and headings.

Is my text uploaded?
No. All conversion happens locally in your browser.
