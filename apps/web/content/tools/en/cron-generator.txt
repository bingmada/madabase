[FAQ]
What is a cron expression?
A cron expression is a string of five fields that defines a schedule. Fields are: minute, hour, day of month, month, and day of week.

What do the five fields mean?
Minute (0-59), Hour (0-23), Day of month (1-31), Month (1-12), Day of week (0-6, Sunday=0).

How do I run a job every hour?
Use 0 * * * * to run at minute 0 of every hour.

How do I run a job every 5 minutes?
Use */5 * * * * to run every 5 minutes.

How do I run a job at 9 AM every weekday?
Use 0 9 * * 1-5 to run at 9 AM Monday through Friday.

What does * mean in cron?
An asterisk (*) means "every" value. * in the month field means every month.

Can I run a job only on specific days?
Yes. Use day constraints like 1-5 for weekdays or specific dates like 1,15 for the 1st and 15th.

Is my schedule uploaded?
No. All cron generation happens locally in your browser.
