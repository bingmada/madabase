[FAQ]
What is JSON escaping?
JSON escaping converts special characters to their escape sequence equivalents. For example, " becomes \", newline becomes \n.

What characters need escaping?
Characters that need escaping include: quotes ("), backslash (\), forward slash (/), backspace, form feed, newline, carriage return, and tab.

Why escape JSON?
Escaping is necessary when inserting JSON strings into code, logs, or other JSON documents to avoid breaking the structure.

What is the difference between escape and encode?
Escape handles JSON-specific special characters. Encode (like base64) converts entire strings to an alternative representation.

Can I unescape JSON?
Yes. The tool can both escape and unescape JSON strings.

Is my text uploaded?
No. All escaping happens locally in your browser.
