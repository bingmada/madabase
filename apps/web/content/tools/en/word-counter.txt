[FAQ]
How does word counter work?
The word counter analyzes your text and counts the number of words. A word is typically defined as a sequence of characters separated by whitespace.

What statistics does it provide?
The tool provides word count, character count (with and without spaces), sentence count, paragraph count, and estimated reading time at various speaking speeds.

What is a good word count for blog posts?
Most blog posts range from 1,000 to 2,500 words. Longer, comprehensive posts tend to rank better in search engines.

How many words per page?
A standard page with about 250 words (12pt, double-spaced) is common for academic writing. Digital content varies widely.

Can I use it for SEO?
Yes. Many SEO tools recommend 300-3,000 words for optimal ranking. Use word count to ensure your content meets recommended lengths.

Does it count characters with spaces?
Yes. The tool shows both character count with spaces and character count without spaces, useful for different contexts like social media limits.

What is estimated reading time?
Reading time is calculated based on average reading speeds. The tool typically uses 200-250 words per minute as a baseline.

Is my text uploaded?
No. All analysis happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.
